The ask · compensation
$54,000→$64,000

This is a request for fair compensation, grounded in contribution and scope — not tenure.

  • $85K+ billed on a $54K salary, and that figure is a conservative floor, not a ceiling.
  • I'm the only web strategist, and there's no web specialist to directly support me.
  • I carry the full weight of web: builds, strategy, training, supervision, client relationships, and company-wide support.
  • No salary adjustment since my promotion in September 2025.
The handbook baseline is 2.5% per year of experience. This ask reflects a genuine expansion in scope and value — and what fair compensation looks like for a role this central, held by one person.
